Best Ticket Resale Sites 2024 to Buy and Sell Concert Tickets

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Who isn’t searching for a side hustle? Whether you’re looking to make a little extra cash selling tickets, or looking for ways to snag amazing seats for a great deal the next time your favorite band or sports team is in town, there’s a wide range of websites out there. Take into consideration things like buyer and seller fees, payment methods that are accepted, countries the website works in, and even customer service, so when you’re having any issue buying or selling, you know you’ll have support. Here’s our list of the best resale sites to buy and sell tickets.

How to choose the best ticket resale sites?

When you’re looking to buy and sell concert tickets, here are a few things to keep in mind.

Seller commissions and buyer fees

If you’re selling, make sure you’re getting a good percentage back. If you’re buying, you want to be sure you’re not going to get slapped with a bunch of unforeseen fees when you go to check out. It’s important to know these rates ahead of time before choosing a website to use.

Refund policy

All kinds of scenarios can happen, like events being canceled, can pop up and create an issue —so knowing the refund policy of the website you purchase the ticket from is helpful.

Checkout fees

Be aware of check-out fees before buying or selling. Knowing these checkout fees and being transparent when selling on the site can help people better plan on seats within their budget.

Availability

You’ll want to use a website with a large number of buyers and sellers. The bigger the audience, the better the chance you’ll sell your tickets. In addition to it being a good thing for the seller, the buyer has a bigger selection from which to choose.

Support

There’s nothing quite as annoying as needing support, whether you’re a buyer or a seller, and being on hold for 20 minutes. Customer service holds more value than people realize. It’s important to have reliable support when buying or selling online.

Maximizing profits: Tips for successfully reselling tickets

To make the most money when reselling tickets, purchase them during presale events. If you purchase when the tickets are the cheapest, and then attempt to sell them before general sales opens to the public, that’s when you'll generally find the most profit.

Avoiding ticket fraud: Tips to safeguard yourself from scams

Unfortunately, there will unfortunately always be people who will try to overcharge and scam buyers. Be sure to read the reviews and even Google the name of the seller to see if anything comes up like cautionary reviews or feedback from other customers.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

Is reselling tickets legal?

You’ll want to make sure that what you’re doing is legal in your state. Reselling tickets is generally okay in the majority of states, but can come with some rules and regulations. Search your state and make sure that you’re staying within regulations and guidelines to avoid any issues.

How much profit can you expect to make reselling tickets?

This depends on a few different variables like the demand for the tickets and the audience you can reach.

What’s the best way to resell tickets fast?

If you’re looking to make fast work of reselling tickets, with a decent return on your initial investment, go for game or concert tickets in high demand if you can, and use sites with a huge audience that have been around for a while, like Ticketmaster.
