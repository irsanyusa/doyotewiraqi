It’s no secret that America’s Got Talent is known for their star studded judging panel. Simon Cowell, Howie Mandel, Heidi Klum, and Sofia Vergara are the perfect judges for the American series, but here’s who I really want to see come to the AGT panel for the new spin off.

When America’s Got Talent: The Champions premiered in 2019 and 2020, the judging panel was painfully similar to AGT. The only difference was the addition of Britain’s Got Talent judge, Alesha Dixon. Now that more international Got Talent members have found their way to America, I think it’s only right that their judges do too.

The official judging cast of AGT All-Stars has officially been confirmed as Howie Mandel, Heidi Klum, and Simon Cowell. This list is based purely on opinion for future seasons.

Got Talent Judges I Would Love to See on AGT All-Stars

1. Alesha Dixon

My dream line up must begin with Britain’s Got Talent and Australia’s Got Talent. It was a fun easter egg to have Dixon in America’s Got Talent: The Champions, so I think she’d be great for this show too. It seems like Dixon isn’t actually apart of the panel this year, since the official cast has been confirmed.

2. Michelle Visage

Ireland’s Got Talent and RuPaul’s Drag Race judge Michelle Visage would be perfect for AGT All-Stars. Her involvement would be legendary, since it would be her first time ever on the American series. Not only would it bring more Ireland’s Got Talent competitors to the AGT stage, it could bring in a whole new (RuPaul’s Drag Race based) fanbase.

3. David Walliams

At this point, I’m begging AGT to bring David Walliams to the USA. If you’ve seen BGT, you know what I mean when I say, Cowell and Walliams is a duo like no other. They need to bring their bromance into the USA.

4. David Foster

This Asia’s Got Talent judge notoriously appeared several times on American Idol, so I’m sure Cowell could make this happen. David Foster has a great reputation of finding and promoting new talent, which in turn would make him a great AGT All-Stars judge.

5. Nicole Scherzinger

It would be great to see current The Masked Singer panelist Nicole Scherzinger return to her Got Talent days. It’s no secret that Scherzinger is one of the most versatile judges on the planet, since she’s appeared on The X Factor UK, The X Factor USA, Australia’s Got Talent, and The Sing Off. I would love to see her alongside Cowell once again.
