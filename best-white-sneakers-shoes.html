How to choose the best white sneakers

Comfort and fit

Comfort is arguably the most crucial factor when it comes to choosing the perfect pair of white sneakers. Not all shoes are created equal—that’s why it’s so important to find a pair that fits you well. For example, if you have wide feet, you’re going to want to avoid a shoe brand that runs narrow. Every shoe brand and style runs differently, so the best way to find a perfectly-fitted pair of sneakers is to try them on in person. While this may be a bit of a pain, it will save you so much time in the long run.

Style

There are so many style options to choose from when it comes to white sneakers. They can range from sporty to chic, and it’s important to consider your individual style when choosing your shoes. You want to make sure you’ll get enough wear out of them, especially if your shoes are more on the expensive side. Choosing a pair of sneakers that can be worn with many different outfits and that compliment your personal style will ensure you get your money’s worth.

Quality

Quality is also such an important factor when choosing a pair of shoes. Let’s be honest, nobody wants to purchase a pair of sneakers they’ll inevitably have to replace in a few months. That’s why it’s a good idea to heavily consider the quality of materials for each sneaker brand before committing. If you’re not looking to wear your shoes often, a pair that’s lower quality may suffice. However, if you’re looking for something to walk long distances every day, you’ll want to opt for a higher quality shoe.

Best way to clean white sneakers

When researching how to clean white shoes, you’ll learn that throwing them in the washing machine and allowing them to air-dry is one of the best options. If your shoes have some dirt spots but aren’t quite to the point of a full wash yet, you can always spot clean them with a Tide Stick or a Mr. Clean Magic Eraser.

How to style white sneakers

White sneakers can be styled in a variety of ways. A chunkier sneaker like the Air Force 1s may go best with a more casual outfit, while a sleek, polished, minimal sneaker like the Common Projects Achilles Low-Top can be styled with dressier outfits. Choose the right pair of shoes and the style possibilities are endless!

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

What can you wear white sneakers with?

White sneakers are super versatile and can be worn with pretty much anything—that’s why we love them! Pair them with jeans, workout gear, dresses, or even slacks. White sneakers always make a statement.

What is the highest selling sneaker brand?

The best selling athletic sneaker brand in the world is currently Nike, with over 50 years in business. They are followed by brands like Adidas and Skechers.

Can you wear white sneakers with work pants?

White sneakers can absolutely go with women’s dress pants for work. It all depends on the sneakers you choose and how you style them. Something sleek and minimal would pair incredibly well with a pair of slacks for a streamlined, modern look.

Why are white sneakers so popular now?

It’s a simple answer, really: they’re classic, versatile, and look good with everything. With numerous brands and styles to choose from, there’s a white sneaker for every person’s unique style.
