I'm Eloping With My New Boyfriend - Dad Finds Prank Goodbye Letter From His 16-Year-Old Daughter

Publish date: 2024-08-31

- The awkward farewell letter a 16-year-old girl left and was picked up by her dad has surfaced on the internet

- In the letter addressed to her parents, the teenager revealed she was 4 months pregnant and had eloped with her new boyfriend

- However, the concluding part of the letter changes everything in a supposed bombshell as it turns out to be a prank

To his surprise, the father found a hand-written note on her bed, The Real Journal reports.

The letter reads:

"I’ve finally found true love, and he’s just great! I especially love his cute piercings, s-exy scars, cool tattoos, and his big motorcycle!

"He’s also explained to me that weed isn’t as bad as some people make it out to be, so now we’re growing 17 plants behind our trailer. We’ll have enough for him and his friends, and in return, they’ll give us as many pills as we want.
