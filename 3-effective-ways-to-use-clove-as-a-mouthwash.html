3 effective ways to use clove as a mouthwash

Publish date: 2024-08-06

This aromatic spice, commonly used in Nigerian cuisine, has been used for centuries as a natural remedy for dental issues, including bad breath, toothache, and gum disease.

Cloves contain a powerful compound called eugenol, which has antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and anaesthetic properties that can help to kill bacteria, reduce inflammation, and numb pain in the mouth.

Below are 3 effective ways to use clove as a mouthwash to keep your mouth feeling clean, healthy, and fresh. These simple and natural remedies can be easily made at home and are perfect for those looking to avoid harsh chemicals and artificial ingredients found in commercial mouthwashes.

1. Clove oil mouthwash

Clove oil is a potent natural antiseptic that can help kill the bacteria that cause bad breath and other oral health problems. To use clove oil as a mouthwash, simply add a few drops of clove oil to a glass of warm water and stir well. Rinse your mouth with this mixture for 30 seconds and then spit it out.

"Using clove oil as a mouthwash is a great way to freshen up your breath and kill harmful bacteria in your mouth." - Dr Aisha, Nigerian dentist.

2. Clove tea mouthwash

ADVERTISEMENT

Clove tea is another effective way to use clove as a mouthwash. To make clove tea, boil a cup of water and add a teaspoon of whole cloves. Let the mixture steep for 10-15 minutes, then strain out the cloves and let the tea cool to room temperature.

Rinse your mouth with the tea, swishing it around for 30 seconds, and then spit it out. "Clove tea mouthwash can help reduce gum inflammation and promote oral health." - Dr Segun, Nigerian dentist.

3. Clove and honey mouthwash

ADVERTISEMENT

Honey is known for its antibacterial properties, and when combined with clove, it can make a potent mouthwash. To make this mouthwash, mix one tablespoon of clove powder with two tablespoons of honey and a cup of warm water. Stir well and use it as a mouthwash.

"The combination of clove and honey can help reduce the bacteria that cause bad breath and improve oral hygiene." - Dr Ife, Nigerian dentist.

Using clove as a mouthwash is an effective way to improve your oral hygiene naturally. Whether you choose to use clove oil, clove tea, or a combination of clove and honey, these natural mouthwashes can help kill harmful bacteria and promote healthy gums and teeth.

So, next time you're looking for a mouthwash, skip the chemical-laden commercial products and try one of these clove mouthwash recipes instead.
