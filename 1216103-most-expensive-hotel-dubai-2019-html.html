Top 8 facts to know about Hotel Atlantis The Palm

  • This fantastic accommodation nestles on the Palm, a human-made island.
  • Its 1539 elegantly-appointed rooms and 7 signature suites provide ultimate luxury, opulence, and spectacular views on the Arabian Sea, the surrounding area, and Dubai skyline.
  • It is a real culinary destination. There are 23 awarded restaurants, serving extraordinary delicacies, refined white and red wine, delicious cheesecakes, and the best seafood.
  • The hotel has its own marine and water park facilities, which include the following: the Sea Lion Point, the Lost Chambers Aquarium, the Aquaventure Waterpark, the Dolphin Bay, facilities for diving, and educational programs.
  • On the territory of Atlantis The Palm, you can try rejuvenating body treatments in the Shuiqi Spa, keep your body fit at the Shuiqi fitness center or visit the beauty salon.
  • During a stay in the hotel, you can request for a private helicopter tour, yacht and boat cruises.
  • In 2016, the hotel got Haute Grandeur Global Hotel Award for being “The Best Hotel in the Middle East”.
  • The same year, this resort got an award “2016 Best Family Resort in the Gulf”.
