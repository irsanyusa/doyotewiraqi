These Male Pink Robins From Australia Are Likely the Brightest and Roundest Birds Youve Ever Seen

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Pink robins are little round, tubby birds that look like they’ve just had a hearty dinner at their grandma’s house. While female pink robins are brownish-black and look like many other birds, their male counterparts know how to steal the show: they have breasts with a saturated pinkish color and are truly eye-catching.

Pink robins are only found in the southeast part of Australia. Male birds have that gorgeous pink candy color on their breasts while females are olive-brown and black — but they’re just as cute and tubby as their male partners. When these bright birds sit on tree branches, they look just like Christmas ornaments. Because of their perfectly round shape, some people also compare them to balls of black and pink yarn.
