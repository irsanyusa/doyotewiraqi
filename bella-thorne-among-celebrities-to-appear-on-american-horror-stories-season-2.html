The Masked Singer alum Bella Thorne is among the actors joining the cast of American Horror Stories season two. This actress is no stranger from the genre of horror, so we’re sure she’ll be great in this role.

American Horror Stories is coming back to FX with some new characters. For those who may not know, American Horror Stories is a spin-off of the popular series, American Horror Story. The spin-off’s first season premiered in August 2021 on Hulu. The first season of this series received mix reviews from critics, noting that there’s a “lack of consistent narrative” among each installment. Regardless of the mixed reviews, the second season is set to premiere on July 21, 2022.

Cody Fern, Max Greenfield, Nico Greetham, Denis O’Hare and Gabourey Sidibe have reprised their roles from the first season for this installment. Celebrities joining the returning cast are Dominique Jackson, Judith Light, Alicia Silverstone, Bella Thorne, and Quvenzhané Wallis. The newcomer’s roles have yet to be defined on IMDB.

American Horror Stories strives to tell a different story in each episode. Each story connects the spin-off to previous seasons of the popular show American Horror Story. Last season brought fans back to American Horror Story: Murder House, so they cannot wait to make this season’s connection.

What’s Up Next For Bella Thorne

American Horror Stories is not Thorne’s only project at the moment. According to her IMDB page, she has several projects in pre and post production. Her upcoming films (currently in post production) are The Trainer (2023) and Rumble Through The Dark. Both films have yet to release a trailer at this time.

Ahead of this project, Thorne starred in Time is Up with her ex-fiancé Benjamin Mascolo. The romance film follows the characters throughout their journey of living in the moment after an accident. Critics called this film “a forgettable mess.”

American Horror Stories isn’t Thorne’s first horror project. She’s also been apart of Scream: The TV Series, Assassination Nation, Amityville: The Awakening, Keep Watching, and more. We can’t wait to see this actress in yet another horror film.
