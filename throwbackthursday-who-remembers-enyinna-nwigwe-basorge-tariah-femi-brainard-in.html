Who remembers Enyinna Nwigwe, Basorge Tariah, Femi Brainard in 'Bachelors?'

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Who remembers watching Ruke Amata's 'Bachelors?' Not only did the series offer us cute cast, it was also hilarious and entertaining.

The series told the story of four men in their late late 20’s, who were close friends from their undergraduate days.

ADVERTISEMENT

One of them, a wealthy playboy, TJ SAVAGE, offered them a chance to live rent-free in his flat, which also happened to be in same compound with his mother's (Mrs Savage).

MRS SAVAGE played by Vivian Metchie-Adeshina.

TJ SAVAGE played by Basorge Tariah / Eric Anderson – A Casanova and heir to his late estranged father’s cosmetics empire.

TEDDY played by Enyina Nwigwe – A Warri born  agent for an entertainment agency.

BODE played by John Njamah - A melancholic journalist and hopeful novelist, who was always working on a new book.

ADVERTISEMENT

PHILIP played by Femi Brainard – A soft and romantic gentleman.

Others in the series were Joan Unuane, Emma Ayalogu, Empress Njamah, Joan Agabi, Ladi Joy Torty, Zack Amata, Kunle Coker, Constance Owoyemi, Maureen Osuji, PrincessTeni Aofiyebi, Chinedu Amah, SegunArinze, Mbong Amata, Omoni Oboli among other.

Who remembers the soundtrack "I'm a bachelor for life?" It was indeed an entertaining show, worth staying up late for.
