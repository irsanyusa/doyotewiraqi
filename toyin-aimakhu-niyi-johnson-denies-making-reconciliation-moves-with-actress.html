Niyi Johnson denies making reconciliation moves with actress

Publish date: 2024-07-23

According to Johnson, the actress had been the one making moves to ensure that they get back together, and he has proof to back up his claim.

Johnson, who is an actor, mentioned this in an interview with Punch Newspaper'sSaturday Beats.

He said, “This is a blatant lie! In fact, the reverse is even the case. I can’t even remember the last time I spoke to her; it probably was sometime last year.  I have never called her since this controversy started. But she has been the one calling me. I have a lot of evidence to prove this.”

The actor was silent about the chances of re-igniting a romance with Aimakhu, bluntly stating that he will rather not discuss the subject.

“I would rather not talk about it. But I have to tell you that in a few weeks, things will unfold.”

Meanwhile, the actress, in past interviews had revealed her undying love for the actor, also stating that though she had deep affection for him, she does not see them getting back together.
