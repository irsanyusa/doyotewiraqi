It’s been about a month since Megan McKenna was crowned the winner of X Factor: Celebrity. She won a record deal with Syco, but she’s also setting her sights on the world of lingerie.

Last month, Megan took to Instagram to announced a new collaboration with lingerie brand Boux Avenue. She posed for some sexy photos as part of the new campaign.

Megan McKenna Models Lingerie

As Megan shared, she’s the face of Boux Avenue’s Christmas campaign, which features an edit of her favorite pieces called Megan Loves Boux. You see more photos of Megan modeling the pieces on BouxAvenue.com.

It’s not all lacy underwear, though. Megan has also modeled the brand’s comfy pajama sets, which she advertised on her Instagram page. And if you’re more interested in her music, don’t worry — she’s working on that too.

In December, Megan posted a photo of herself in the studio, writing, “2020 can’t come soon enough.” She’s also been posting covers of songs like “Lose You to Love Me” by Selena Gomez.

Megan’s ‘X Factor’ Journey

Megan is known for her appearances on reality shows like Celebrity Big Brother and The Only Way Is Essex. She also made it to the semifinals of Britain’s Got Talent in 2009 as part of the duo Harmony.

Megan has been pursuing a career in music for years, and even auditioned for the regular version of X Factor in 2014 and 2015. She released her debut album Story of Me in 2018 and went on a tour of the UK.

The singer’s talent carried her through the first season of X Factor: Celebrity in 2019. She was named the winner with more than 46 percent of the vote, and performed original song “This” during the finale.
