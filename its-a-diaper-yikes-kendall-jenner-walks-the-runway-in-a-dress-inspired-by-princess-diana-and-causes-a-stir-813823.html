Its a Diaper, Yikes: Kendall Jenner Walks the Runway in a Dress Inspired by Princess Diana

Publish date: 2024-07-03

“Oh, a walking marshmallow,” commented one user. As the saying goes, dress to impress, but this time the overly stylish choice backfired. Instead of receiving admiring looks and astonished gasps, Kendall Jenner was the object of many jokes on social media.

She showed off her look at a recent show.

Laurent VU/SIPA/Sipa Press/East News, Laurent VU/SIPA/Sipa Press/East News

The Jacquemus fashion show at Chateau de Versailles will be remembered for sure just because of one dress. On Monday, Kendall Jenner walked the runway wearing a fluffy and cloud-like white dress. Her look was finished off with drop earrings and a choker similar to the one Princess Diana wore.

The dress was inspired by the royalty.

The whole collection was inspired by Princess Diana’s iconic fashion. The show was called Le Chouchou as a reference to both Marie Antoinette and Princess Diana. The audience was sitting in cream and white row boats watching the autumn looks that Simon Porte Jacquemus created.

The dress was compared to a diaper.

As soon as the model shared the photos on Instagram, her fans immediately left amusing comments. Someone commented, “Is this fashion? Because it looks like a diaper. Yikes.” Another added, “Didn’t realize that wearing an adult diaper was considered to be fashion. You learn something new everyday.”

People started other amusing comparisons.

Apart from diapers, the dress also reminded people of a big marshmallow, scrunchie, shower cap, walking cloud, and even a pillow. Some pointed out, “Looks like big grandma underwear,” “The loofah hanging in my shower.”

Others wondered, “Is it just me or does that look like a toilet roll?”, “Is that the airbag for the submarine?” The dress is like a piece of art that everyone sees differently.

The celebrity world is never boring, so we will always have something to discuss. For example, Doja Cat wore 30,000 crystals and Kylie Jenner chose a lion’s head for the 2023 Schiaparelli couture show.
