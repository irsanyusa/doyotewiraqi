We’ve seen plenty of amazing acrobatic acts on America’s Got Talent over the years, from aerialists to balancing groups. It’s always refreshing to see these types of acts go far in the competition, since the show is about more talents than just singing. Let’s take a look at the best of the best acrobats in AGT history.

The Best Acrobatic Acts on America’s Got Talent

1. Duo Transcend

This husband and wife trapeze duo auditioned for America’s Got Talent in Season 13. They had a scary mishap during Judge Cuts, but they later successfully repeated the dangerous routine in the Quarterfinals. Duo Transcend made it all the way to the finale, and they later returned for AGT: The Champions, finishing in second place.

2. Aidan Bryant

This self-taught aerialist auditioned for Season 16 at just 16 years old. He shared that he started practicing acrobatics with a sheet hanging from his grandmother’s tree. Aidan proved that you don’t need years of professional training to master a talent, as he finished the season in second place and inspired Simon Cowell to stand on the judges’ desk during the finale.

3. Alan Silva

This aerialist comes from a talented family, and his brother Alfredo previously competed as part of Deadly Games. Alan continually wowed the judges in Season 15 with his dramatic aerial routines. In the finale, he incorporated both fire and water into his soaring outdoor performance, which took him to fifth place for the season.

4. V.Unbeatable

This acrobatic dance group from India stunned everyone in Season 14 with their energetic performances that seemed to defy gravity. After getting the Golden Buzzer from guest judge Dwyane Wade, the group finished in fourth place. Later, they returned for AGT: The Champions and were chosen as the winners in an amazing comeback victory.

5. Sofie Dossi

This contortionist was just 14 years old when she auditioned for Season 11 of AGT. Sofie showed off amazing contortion, hand balancing, aerial, and even foot archery skills throughout the competition. After earning a Golden Buzzer from guest judge Reba McEntire, she made it to the finale and later returned to compete in AGT: The Champions.

6. Bello Sisters

These three Italian-German sisters from a circus family auditioned for Season 15 of America’s Got Talent with an incredible balancing act in which they put themselves into seemingly impossible positions. They leveled up their performances from week to week throughout the competition and made it all the way to the finale.

7. Rialcris

These three brothers were born in Colombia and lived in Mexico for two decades. They auditioned for the show in Season 16 with an impressive balancing act that showed off their amazing strength. The trio made it all the way to the Semifinals. Although they didn’t go to the finale, they left a powerful impression on the series with their talent.

8. Zurcaroh

This acrobatic dance group was originally founded in Brazil. They wowed everyone with their audition, which featured incredible balancing and gymnastics moves. The group earned the Golden Buzzer from host Tyra Banks and ended up finishing Season 13 in second place after weeks of awe-inspiring performances.
