We’re used to seeing Kylie Jenner all made up, but quarantine has brought out a more casual side to her. She was recently photographed going makeup-free and wearing a comfy set of sweats to visit her friend.

Of course, The Daily Mail points out that Kylie seems to be ignoring her own advice about staying home during the coronavirus pandemic. She was snapped by paparazzi visiting her friend Stassie Karanikolaou this week.

Kylie Jenner Goes Makeup-Free And Wears Sweats

In the above photos, Kylie can be seen going barefoot in a loose-fitting pair of tie-dye sweatpants and matching sweatshirt. Her hair is pulled back into a messy bun, and she doesn’t appear to be wearing makeup.

Kylie also had a bag of kettle-cooked potato chips in her arms as she headed to her car. But don’t be fooled by the casual look. The Daily Mail reports that Kylie’s outfit (from the brand Amiri) costs over $1,000. She may not look much like a billionaire here, but she definitely is one.

Last month, Kylie posted an Instagram selfie from home, captioning the picture, “bored in the house and i’m in the house bored.”

What Do Fans Think Of Kylie’s Natural Look?

Fans on Twitter were surprised by Kylie’s casual new look. One user said she “looks cute” with no makeup on, while another said she looks like her former self. Another fan tweeted, “I prefer this Kylie Jenner.”

Kylie obviously loves makeup, and has her own cosmetics line — which had to halt production during the pandemic. But she’s also changed her appearance through fillers.

Last year, Kylie told Paper magazine that she’s never had any major procedures done, contrary to some people’s speculation. “I’m terrified! I would never,” she said. “They don’t understand what good hair and makeup and, like, fillers, can really do.”
