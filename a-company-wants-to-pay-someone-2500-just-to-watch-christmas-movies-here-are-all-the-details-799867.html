A Company Wants to Pay Someone $2,500 Just to Watch Christmas Movies. Here Are All the Details

Publish date: 2024-07-12

If you’ve ever wished that you could get paid to simply lie in bed and watch your favorite Christmas movies, your prayers have been answered. Now, while watching your December marathon of merry movies, a company is willing to pay you more than enough to get your Christmas presents bought and wrapped.

We at Bright Side are celebrating the joy of Christmas and would like to share more details about this exciting opportunity with our readers.

Your love for Christmas movies is needed to find the best one.

In order to find the best Christmas movie of all time, Reviews.org is willing to hire a Christmas lover as their “Chief Holiday Cheermeister” to perform a special task. Your role will be to find the time to watch 25 Christmas movies in 25 days, and complete a post-movie short survey reviewing all of them.

You will be paid handsomely for your time. Almost TOO handsomely.

At $100 per movie, by the end of your task, you’ll get a check for $2,500 from the company. Not only that, but you’ll also receive a year subscription to Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, Amazon Prime, HBO Max, Apple TV+, and Hallmark Movies Now.

You can pick the movies you want to watch.

As a recommendation, the company has suggested titles like — Home Alone, Frosty the Snowman, The Santa Clause, Elf, Klaus, A Christmas Story, Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer, among others. But, it is totally up to you if you want to watch a timeless classic or the latest release on streaming services.

Here’s how you can apply:

If you are over 18, just head over and fill out this form here, filling in your details and answering “Why do you feel you’re the right person for the Holiday Movie Dream Job?” The applications will close on December 4, 2020, so you’d better hurry.
