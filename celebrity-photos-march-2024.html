Nicholas Galitzine Premieres Mary and George; Tyler, The Creator Launches His Louis Vuit

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Kim Kardashian, Lisa Rinna, Salma Hayek, Cher, Saweetie, Julia Fox and Ashley Park were just some of the celebs who flocked to Paris at the beginning of March for Paris Fashion Week. Sam Smith even walked in the Vivienne Westwood show, where they went almost unrecognizable in a booty-baring ensemble.

In New York, Millie Bobby Brown premiered her new Netflix film, “Damsel,” posing for photographers with her fiancé Jake Bongiovi and his parents Jon Bon Jovi and Dorothea Hurley. At the same time, in Los Angeles, Jack Black, Bryan Cranston, Awkwafina and James Hong had some fun on the carpet at the premiere of “Kung Fu Panda 4.”

And, of course, Hollywood was gearing up for the Academy Awards with plenty of Oscar-related celebrating and toasting. (You can see plenty of those photos here.)
