'AGT' Ventriloquists Terry Fator, Celia Muoz Impress in Spectacular Michael Jackson Duet

Publish date: 2024-06-26

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photos by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Terry Fator and Celia Muñoz gave fans an early holiday treat with a stunning duet of a Michael Jackson classic. The America’s Got Talent season two winner collaborated with Muñoz to sing a Spanish cover of “I Just Can’t Stop Loving You.“

Terry Fator, Celia Muñoz Sing a Rendition of “I Just Can’t Stop Loving You” by Michael Jackson

Recently, Fator collabed with Muñoz for a striking rendition of Jackson’s 1987 smash hit on YouTube. Belting out the song’s Spanish cover “Todo Mi Amor Eres,” the two showed off their incredible voices.

During their performance, Fator used his Michael Jackson puppet while impersonating Jackson’s voice. He began singing the first verse and Muñoz revealed herself to sing along.

Upon listening to the cover, many fans took their delighted reactions to the comment section.

“Both is a[n] explosion of talent and beauty! Our king Michael would be so proud. Keep doing Micheals performances please we love it,” one fan commented.

“Puppet Michael Jackson is just too cute for words and singing my favorite song in Spanish…perfect. Love all of your characters, Terry. You’ve got an awesome act,” another fan wrote.

In May, Fator and Muñoz sent their fans into a frenzy after they subtly teased a potential collaboration. As seen on Muñoz’s Instagram Story post, she is seated at a table with Fator. At the time, no specifics were mentioned yet.

He Previously Did a Cover of Michael Jackson’s “Ben”

It’s not first time that Fator impressed with a Michael Jackson ventriloquist cover. Weeks ago, the AGT season two winner sang Jackson’s “Ben” for Halloween. Like usual, many fans expressed their appreciation for Fator’s talent upon hearing his cover.

“Back in the day, I found myself relating to the little rat in the song. For that reason, the song and Michael hold special place in my heart. Fantastic interpretation, Terry,” a person wrote in the comments.

“You have an insanely incredible voice,” another user wrote.

At present, Fator is headlining a show in Sin City, Who’s The Dummy Now? He’s back on stage after his record-breaking residency at The Mirage.

“Who’s the Dummy Now? is a comedic and musical ‘origin story,’ in which we explore what makes Terry tick, and how he came up with the puppets who are his partners on stage,” the description in his website read.
