The Masked Singer Season 8 is about to start filming, and fans can request tickets now to sit in the audience. If you attend a taping, you’ll also have the opportunity to vote for your favorite contestants in the new competition.

How to Get Tickets for The Masked Singer Season 8

Fans can request free tickets to The Masked Singer Season 8 tapings here. Tapings begin on Sunday, July 24, at Red Studios in Los Angeles, California. Attendees must be at least 12 years old, and Covid-19 rapid tests will be administered before seating the audience.

According to the ticket site, this season will feature “shorter tapings for an amazing fun filled fan experience.” In addition to seeing this season’s new characters and vote for their favorites, fans will also apparently have the chance to meet past characters and win prizes.

When requesting tickets, you’ll need to provide your name and some basic information such as date of birth and email address. You’ll also be asked to share if you’re fully vaccinated. Covid-19 protocols will apparently be on the event ticket.

Some of this season’s Masked Singer episodes will have themes. For example, on July 28, the show will be taping Muppet Night, and fans are asked to dress in pink or green. Next, on July 29, the show will tape TV Theme Show Night.

When Does Season 8 Premiere?

The Masked Singer Season 8 is scheduled to premiere on FOX on Wednesday, September 21. The new season of LEGO Masters is also set to premiere that night after initially being scheduled for May 31 of this year.

It will be a busy week for premieres, since The Voice premieres on Monday, September 19, on NBC. Dancing with the Stars Season 31 is also rumored to be premiering on that date on Disney+. Disney+ has yet to announce an official premiere date at this time.

We can’t wait to find out more about Season 8 of The Masked Singer, and to finally meet the contestants who will be competing for the trophy. We’re excited to see the different costumes brought to the stage this season.
